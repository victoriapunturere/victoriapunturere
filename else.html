Else, 2018
02.03.18 & 03.03.18, 7pm
170 Abbotsford Street North Melbourne

“oomph!,
manipulated control,
touch me,
strung…”

A performance piece directed by Victoria Punturere and Dasha Tolotchov, that explores the connection between body, space and movement through choreography, material and sound. Presented at Gallerysmith in Melbourne, March 2018.

Dancers: Charlie Lloyd, Anna Tolotchkov, Isabella Punturere & Olivia Punturere.
