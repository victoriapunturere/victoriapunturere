Information
Victoria’s spatial practice embraces science and technology to propose speculative projects that enter the science fiction realm. Driven by an interest for human and non-human science, her experiments unfold through a utilisation of space, body, object, material, film and performance. With a background in classical and contemporary ballet, Victoria’s projects draw a connection between performance and space in narrating immersive spatial experiences.

Victoria’s practice employs a critical approach in raising a consciousness to question and guide the direction of future spaces and landscapes. Through her interdisciplinary approach, Victoria’s projects endeavour in pushing the limits of design and architecture.
